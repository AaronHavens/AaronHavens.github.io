About me

I am currently a PhD student studying the interplay of tools in control theory, optimization and machine learning under Professor Bin Hu. In particular, I am interested in using control theoretic stability and safety conditions (such as LMIs) to regularize the learned controller solutions and sometimes guarantee system properties.
